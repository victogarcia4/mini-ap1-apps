Interactive Anatomy Explorer

A study tool for regional anatomy, body planes and sections, directional terminology, and the abdominopelvic quadrants — all referenced to the standard anatomical position.

Anatomical Position: The subject stands erect and faces forward, with feet parallel and flat, arms at the sides, and palms facing forward (supinated). Every directional term is defined relative to this reference posture — “left” and “right” always mean the subject’s left and right.
