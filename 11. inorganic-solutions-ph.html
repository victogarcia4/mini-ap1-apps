The pH Scale (0–14)

0 acidic7 neutral14 basic
7.0 Neutral Equal H⁺ and OH⁻

Why Buffers Matter

A buffer resists changes in pH. Add the same acid to both beakers and watch the difference — this is how blood holds pH near 7.4.
