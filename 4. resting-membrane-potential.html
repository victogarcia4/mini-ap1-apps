Resting Membrane Potential & the Na⁺/K⁺ ATPase Pump

Interactive physiology simulation — leak channels, the electrogenic pump & the Nernst equation

🧸 The simple version
The cell is like a tiny battery with a minus sign inside. Little doors let potassium (purple) sneak out, which makes the inside more negative. A pump keeps recharging the battery by pushing 3 sodiums out and pulling 2 potassiums in, using food energy (ATP, green) as fuel. Add too much potassium outside and the battery gets weaker (dangerous!). Take too much away and it gets stronger.
