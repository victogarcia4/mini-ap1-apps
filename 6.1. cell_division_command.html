CELL DIVISION COMMAND

Guide the cell through the cell cycle. Manage resources, pass checkpoints, and produce healthy daughter cells.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Check Your Tasks: Look at the Task Queue at the bottom of the screen to see what needs to happen next.
  2. Interact: Click the organelles and structures inside the center cell visualization in the correct biological order matching your tasks.
  3. Manage Resources: Actions cost ATP and Nucleotides. Clicking out of order or running out of resources drains Cell Health.
  4. Pass Checkpoints: Use your biological knowledge to pass quiz checkpoints between major phases.
  5. Survive: Don't let your Cell Health reach zero, or the cell will undergo Apoptosis (programmed cell death)!
