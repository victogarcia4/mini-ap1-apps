G1 Phase (First Gap)

The cell undergoes rapid growth and active metabolism. Organelles like centrioles begin to replicate in preparation for future division. The cell functions normally while accumulating necessary building blocks.

Deep Dive: Checkpoints

G1/S Checkpoint (Restriction Point): The cell evaluates if it is large enough, has sufficient nutrients, and if DNA is undamaged before committing to DNA replication (S phase). If conditions are not met, the cell may enter a resting state (G0) or undergo apoptosis.
