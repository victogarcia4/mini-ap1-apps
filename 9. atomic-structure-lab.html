Compare & Contrast — the 5 terms

TermWhat it isWhich particle change causes it
IonAtom with a net electric chargeGain/lose electrons (protons unchanged)
ElectrolyteIon dissolved in water that conducts electricityAn ion (charged) placed in solution
Free radicalAtom/molecule with an unpaired electron — highly reactiveAn odd/unpaired electron in the valence shell
IsotopeSame element, different massChange the number of neutrons
RadioisotopeAn unstable isotope that emits radiation as it decaysAn unstable neutron:proton ratio
