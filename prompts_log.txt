ANATOMICAL IMAGE ARCHITECTURE & FALLBACK SVG SPECIFICATION
==========================================================

This document logs the 12 medical-grade DALL-E 3 image prompts required for Chapter 8: Joints, alongside their fallback Python SVG generation scripts or rendering blueprints for clean schematic diagrams.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. General Synovial Joint Structure
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DALL-E 3 Prompt:
"Anatomical illustration of a typical synovial joint cross-section, showing two articulating bone ends, articular hyaline cartilage covering bone ends, a double-layered joint capsule enclosing a synovial cavity, fibrous layer continuous with periosteum, and a loose connective tissue synovial membrane lining. High detail, labeled parts style, medical textbook aesthetic, isolated on a clean white background."

Fallback SVG Generation Blueprint:
- Draw two vertical bone ends using round-rect shapes, colored bone-white (#FAF5E8) with a grey outline.
- Add light blue caps (#B6E2FD) on both bone ends to represent articular cartilage.
- Draw a double-layered enclosing capsule using curves (#115E59) joining the periosteum of both bones.
- Inner layer colored pink (#FDA4AF) for the synovial membrane.
- Fill the cavity with transparent light teal (#E0F2FE).
- Draw labels with lines pointing to: Articular Cartilage, Joint Capsule, Synovial Membrane, Synovial Cavity.
